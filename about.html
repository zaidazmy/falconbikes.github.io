FALCON Bikes (Pvt) Ltd.

  • Home
  • About

Launched in Sri Lanka 20 years ago, the Tomahawk brand has taken the island by storm, re-defining adventure and making cycling desirable again. With stylish and contemporary designs, Tomahawk has become a household name synonymous with adventure and exceptional experience. With a wide range of bicycles made for different age groups, Tomahawk bicycles offer high standards in safety and undeniable quality, with 60% of market share and the number one position in the market. With an 11-year warranty period, the brand is known for its unsurpassed after-sales service.
